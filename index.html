Korg Monologue


Watch this playlist on YouTube

The Monologue is a great, inexpensive 2 oscillator monosynth with a incredible 16-step sequencer built in - complete with motion sequencing. A killer synth for beginners and pros alike, with a great balance of surface "grab the knob tweakability" and deep under the surface editing.

You can watch this series on my YouTube channel.

Live Jams


Watch this playlist on YouTube

Live jams are the whole reason I started the channel. I wanted to force myself to create music using my synths on a far more regular basis and reflect on what I created.

"Going DAWless" is one of the best strategies I've found for breaking writers block and sharpening my musical instincts and I can highly recommend it.

You can watch this series on my YouTube channel.
